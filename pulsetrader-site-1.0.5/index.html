How It Works

From breaking story to signal in minutes

PulseTrader doesn't try to beat algorithms. It helps you understand what a story means before the broader market has fully digested it.

01
Continuous Scanning
Our cloud server monitors 24+ RSS feeds, SEC filings, Fed speeches, and YouTube press conferences — around the clock, every 5 minutes.
02
AI Analysis
Each story is scored by Claude AI for sentiment, strength, and news candle probability. Complex stories get a plain-English summary of what it means.
03
Signal in Your Overlay
Signals push directly to your overlay via WebSocket in real time. High-probability candle alerts fire audio and visual notifications instantly.
